Return Policy

At Aviatoras, we value your satisfaction and aim to make your shopping experience effortless. Below are the guidelines for initiating a return:

1. Return Conditions:

Products must be returned in their original, unused condition, with all tags and packaging intact. Customized items can be returned only if they are defective or damaged upon receipt.

2. Return Period:

You have 24 days from the delivery date to initiate a return. Please contact our customer support promptly to initiate the process.

3. Steps to Return:

Begin by emailing our customer support to request a return authorization code. Securely pack the item in its original box, including all accessories and documentation, and affix the provided authorization code. Use a traceable mail service for return shipping. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except in cases of our error.

4. Refunds and Exchanges:

Upon receiving your returned item and verifying its condition, we will process your refund or exchange. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically take 5-7 business days to reflect. Exchanges are subject to product availability. If an item is unavailable, we may offer a replacement or refund. Please note that original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

5. Handling Damaged or Incorrect Items:

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact our customer service immediately with photographs and details. We will cover return shipping costs for such items and provide a refund or replacement as necessary.

6. Dealing with Damaged or Incorrect Items:

Certain items, such as hygiene-sensitive products like swimwear, are non-returnable for sanitary reasons. Please refer to specific product pages for details or reach out to our customer support for clarification.
