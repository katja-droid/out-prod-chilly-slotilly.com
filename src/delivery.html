Delivery Information

Welcome to Chilly-Slotilly.com! We are committed to delivering your dog clothes and accessories swiftly and securely. Here’s all the information you need about our shipping policies:

1. Order Processing Time

Once you place an order, we begin processing it immediately. Processing usually takes between 2 to 10 business days, depending on the current order volume and any ongoing promotions. We’ll keep you updated throughout the process.

2. Shipping Times and Procedures

Shipping times vary by product and are specified on each product page, giving you a clear estimate of when your order will arrive.

3. Warranty Policy

If you find any defects covered by our warranty, please contact our support team within the warranty period and provide your receipt. We’ll assess the product to determine if it qualifies for warranty coverage.

4. Handling Warranty Claims

For valid warranty claims, we will repair or replace the product at our discretion. If the original item is unavailable, we’ll substitute it with one of equal or greater value. The original purchase's warranty period applies to all repairs or replacements.

5. Warranty Exclusions

Our warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage due to misuse, accidental or intentional
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Failure to follow maintenance instructions
  • External events like accidents or natural disasters

6. Order Confirmation

To avoid delays, please ensure your shipping details are accurate. Double-check all information during checkout.

7. Return Process

If your shipment is returned due to incorrect contact information or if you’re unavailable to receive it, we’ll contact you to arrange another delivery. This may involve additional fees.

8. Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions based on their nature or destination. We’ll notify you of any restrictions and suggest alternatives if necessary.

For further details or assistance, please contact our customer support at support@chilly-slotilly.com. We’re committed to ensuring your order arrives promptly and accurately.
