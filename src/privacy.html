Privacy Guidelines

Welcome to Aviatoras, where safeguarding your privacy is as crucial as charting your next adventure. Below, we outline our steadfast commitment to protecting your data and the meticulous protocols governing its handling when you engage with our services and website.

Data Collection Expedition:

Personal Flight Logs: When you embark on transactions, we collect vital details like your name, email, travel address, and payment particulars.

Skyline Interactions: We track your journey through our website using technologies like flight cookies, capturing your digital footprint including your IP altitude and browser type.

2. Data Utilization Navigations:

Flight Path Processing: Your personal data powers our operations, enabling us to navigate orders and maintain seamless communication throughout your journey with us.

In-Flight Communications: We harness your contact coordinates to dispatch confirmations and provide updates on your journey with Aviatoras.

Tailored Experiences: With your consent, we customize your journey based on past interactions and preferences, ensuring each flight with us is uniquely tailored to you.

Continuous Navigation Enhancements: By analyzing collected data, we continuously refine our services, ensuring your journey with Aviatoras remains smooth and enjoyable.

3. Information Disclosure:

Flight Crew Collaboration: Your data may be shared with trusted partners under strict privacy agreements to assist in operations such as logistics and payment processing, ensuring your journey remains seamless from takeoff to landing.

Legal Flight Paths: We may disclose your data if required by law to protect our rights, secure our services, or ensure the safety of our passengers and crew.

4. Security Shields:

Our security protocols are meticulously engineered to shield your personal information from unauthorized access and misuse. However, it's essential to recognize that flying data online carries inherent risks.

5. Your Privacy Choices:

Opt-Out Turbulences: You retain control over your journey. You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time using the links provided in our messages or by contacting our flight control directly.

Cookie Altitudes: You can manage your cookie preferences via your web browser settings, ensuring your digital altitude is always under your control.

6. Child Safety:

We do not knowingly collect data from passengers under the age of 16. If such data is inadvertently collected, it will be swiftly expunged from our systems.

7. Policy Updates:

Our Privacy Pledge undergoes periodic updates to adapt to changing skies. We encourage you to review our policy regularly, as your continued journey with Aviatoras signifies your acceptance of any changes.

For inquiries or further details regarding our privacy practices, please reach out to our flight control at info@Aviatoras.com. We are dedicated to addressing your concerns promptly and upholding your privacy rights as you soar through the skies with us.
